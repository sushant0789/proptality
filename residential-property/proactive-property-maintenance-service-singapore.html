Proactive Property Maintenance Service

Every property must undergo regular proactive property inspection service to find property issues at the starting stage itself. At Proptality, we offer such services to property owners. Entrust your property to the best Property Engineers in town.

Under this Service, we offer:
  • 1. A comprehensive site audit of the property includes an external property audit and an internal audit.
  • 2.Recommend corrective actions and rectify the issues.
Scope of External Property Audit:
  • 1. Cracks on External Structure
  • 2. Leaking
  • 3. Vegetation growth on the structure
  • 4. Bird attack, Termite attack
  • 5. Paint Quality
Scope of Internal Property Audit
  • 1. Pipes, Fitting, Faucets, toilet flush systems.
  • 2. Lights, Fan, switches, sockets, DB check
  • 3. Door, window, window grills, hinges, handles, locks,
  • 4. Strains on Floor, Walls, Moulds on the ceiling, Dampness of Wall, etc
  • 5. Electrical equipment like Aircons, Water Heater, Hood.
Who do we recommend for such a Service?

We recommend this Property Inspection / Property Audit service for tenanted properties only. A landlord who stays in their property always takes care of their property promptly. In contrast, tenanted properties don't enjoy such attention from Tenants. So you must engage us to review your property regularly to identify upcoming significant issues at the very beginning itself.
